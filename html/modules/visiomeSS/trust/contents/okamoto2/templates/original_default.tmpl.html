Interactive Illustration of Hough Transform: y = a x + b

[Usage of the applet]

Click on the (x, y) plane: draw a straight line, b = - x0 a + y0, on the (a, b) plane.

Click on the (a, b) plane: draw a straight line, y = a0 x + b0, on the (x, y) plane.

If you click a set of points on the (x, y) plane along the line, y = a0 x + b0, then the lines on the (a, b) plane will intersect at the point (a0, b0).
